About Us

Beirut Duty Free’s reputation is rapidly growing as one of the region finest duty free shops, delivering an exceptional travel retail experience through our core retail philosophy focusing on customer service, quality of choice and price. Our comprehensive range of products available in a carefully crafted retail environment is available to all visitors passing through Beirut Rafic Hariri International Airport at any time of day or night. Come and see for yourself and experience great value and exceptional service.

Our History

Beirut has regained its former splendor, elegance and sophistication. As part of the program of national rejuvenation, Beirut Rafic Hariri International Airport has emerged as a modern icon of the rebirth and the reconstruction of Beirut.

Within this new and modern terminal building, Beirut Duty Free has set about creating one of the finest duty free retail environments in the world and has succeeded in establishing itself as a brand in a highly competitive region of duty free operators.

Beirut Duty Free is managed and controlled by Phoenicia Aer Rianta Company. Beirut Duty Free wants passengers to enjoy the airport more and to sample new products and of course we would be happy if after that you wanted to buy. The airport should be fun, whether its the start of your journey or the end you should enjoy and as we say " Take back more..."

Our Mission

Our company’s mission is to design, deliver and develop the finest retail experience and value, for all our customers through the execution of business excellence and driving performance for the benefit of all our stakeholders.
